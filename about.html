About

Hi, I’m Lauren Ciulla.

I am a multifaceted designer based in New York City. Combining a passion for creativity with experience in visual design, frontend development, user research, and content creation, I help clients express themselves and their ideas through digital content.

I’m currently a Visual Designer with IBM’s CIO Design team. I incorporate graphic design, animation, user experience design, and original content creation to make an impact on products and services IBMers use every day.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude from the University of Connecticut Honors Program in 2020 with a dual degree. I earned a BA in Digital Media & Design with a Web & Interactive Media concentration, and a BA in English. During my time at UConn, I completed internships in design, development, and marketing, all while working on various student-run agile teams that produced work for the Digital Media & Design department.

Enthusiastic about content creation, problem solving, and digital communication, I am always eager to hear about new opportunities.
