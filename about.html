About

Hi, I’m Lauren Ciulla.

I'm a designer based in New York City. Combining experience in visual, multimedia, and UX design with a passion for creativity and getting teams to thrive, I help clients express themselves and their ideas through digital content.

I’m currently a Visual Designer with IBM’s CIO Design team. I incorporate motion graphics, UX/UI design, illustration, and Design Thinking to make an impact on products and services IBMers use every day.

I’ve worked on a variety of projects, but my favorites are ones I can fully dive into, develop something from the ground up, and learn something new in the process. I’m dedicated to continuously deepening my skills and experimenting with new mediums.

Enthusiastic about content creation, problem solving, and digital communication, I am always eager to hear about new opportunities.
