Aetna Undergraduate Writing in the Disciplines Honorable Mention

My essay titled “Make an Impression: Emotional Realism in the Works of Thomas Hardy and Virginia Woolf” explores how Hardy and Woolf diverge from traditional realism to uncover the emotional realities of individuals through exploratory literary conventions, like focalization and creative narration styles. It was recognized in the humanities division of Aetna’s 2020 Writing in the Disciplines Awards, and featured in the third annual Aetna Celebration of Student Writing.
