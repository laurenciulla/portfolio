Design Thinking

I am the lead visual designer for a modern costing and pricing application. The tool is in development and upon release will improve the work experience of IBM’s services pricing community.

I jumpstarted the Design Thinking effort on the team, hosting workshops and brainstorming sessions to align us to one core mission: making work better for IBM’s services pricing community. In addition to advocating for Agile methodologies and leading with design, I put those beliefs into practice through my work on the application.

Throughout the design process, I collaborate with the team’s user experience designer to produce effective designs based on stakeholder priorities, input from our subject matter experts, and a constant flow of user feedback. I ensure the designs adhere to IBM’s design system and facilitate the handoff to development. I also implemented a system for quality assurance and maintain an active line of communication with the development team to foster alignment.

I have also taken responsibility for the promotional communication strategy. Using IBM’s brand guidelines, I designed an app icon and branding style that are utilized on the application’s website. I also created a promotional video to spread the word about the new tool.

The video was my favorite project so far–I facilitated a brainstorming session to understand the message we wanted to send with it, then wrote a script and mapped out a storyboard. After a few iterations of each, I dove into the animation. The video garnered excitement for the app’s release and boosted traffic to the website.
