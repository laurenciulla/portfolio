Graphic Design

/ /

Illustration

These screensavers appear on digital signage throughout IBM CIO offices. They give the offices a friendly, welcoming feel that aligns with the CIO Design team’s vibrant style and culture.

A few of the illustrations aim to welcome employees back to the office after COVID-19 closures. They remind people to practice social distancing and help to reestablish a sense of community.
